## Presentation Tips

### Delivery Guidelines

1. **Timing:** Keep presentation to 15-20 minutes, allow 10 minutes for Q&A
2. **Visuals:** Use high-quality images, diagrams, and mockups
3. **Storytelling:** Focus on problem-solution narrative
4. **Data:** Include real market data and projections
5. **Demo:** If possible, show live demo of MVP features
6. **Engagement:** Ask questions and interact with audience

### Slide Design Recommendations

* Use consistent color scheme (BPA brand colors)
* Keep text minimal, use visuals
* Use icons and infographics
* Maintain professional look
* Ensure readability from distance
